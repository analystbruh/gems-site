Programs

Money Matters

INVEST in YOUR FUTURE!

Have you ever heard adults say things like “Adulting is Hard”, or “I wish I knew about finances when I was younger”? Well, here's an opportunity for our young people to gain basic banking knowledge and how to apply finances to everyday life. In these sessions our young leaders will learn how money and career aspirations align. Along with building the strategy on how to obtain and manage finances to pursue academic and life goals.

Career Pathways

CREATE OPPORTUNITY for YOURSELF!

The WORLD is YOURS is what most students hear throughout their lives. Knowing what you want to do once you finish high school could be a big blur with so many options to choose from. Our Career Pathways program gives opportunities for individuals to explore various industries and career paths to help identify interests and learn more about the careers options available. Participants in the program will have the opportunity to build networking skills by meeting and engaging with successful industry professionals, including the path to entrepreneurship.

SpeakLife

YOUR WORDS…YOUR VOICE!

SpeakLife is designed to provide an outlet for the voice of our youth to be heard in a positive light! Express yourself in creative writing projects whether it's music, poetry, short stories, and/or social impact topic discussions. Using your voice to engage is important not only to grow communication skills but also to learn to express feelings on matters of interest and situations that may impact your life from your perspective. Participants in the program will have the opportunity to feature guests on our SpeakLife podcast and media productions as well as have an opportunity to submit their completed SpeakLife projects for inclusion in our SpeakLife anthology publication and entry to scholarship program.
