A message from our founder…

Being raised in a strong community driven small-town environment, it was always embedded in me to serve and give back. From my early years of assisting in daycare at church as a young teen and later serving in auxiliary ministry in my adult years and as a professional volunteering in corporate employee resource groups, I have and continue my desire to help others. My mother would say GEMS started when I was a kid always looking out for other kids and my desire to teach. I remember during summer and school breaks gathering my younger sister and neighborhood kids to “play school”. Can you imagine, as a kid convincing other kids to use their break time to continue learning, chalkboard and all. From my past to my present, I'm excited to share with you all where the vision of GEMS started from and how it has come to be. I look forward to bringing our services to your students and providing them with GEMS to help them grow and develop into their futures.

Our History

“It takes a village” is a statement that is widely used in the upbringing of our kids. We all know the importance of school and obtaining an education, however there is also a component of meeting the kids where they are, to help them along the way. Knowing there is an area of need to help students succeed, the vision for GEMS Inc. (GEMS) came to fruition and was officially established as a 501c3 nonprofit organization in August 2011. The vision for GEMS is to provide resources and tools for middle and high school students (ages 12-18) in the areas of health, education, arts, and life skills.

After development of the organization structure and planning of programs, in 2014, GEMS Inc. launched its first community outreach, Bay Area Family Literacy Day, providing educational resources and literary works from authors, illustrators and business professionals to students and their families. Since then, GEMS has partnered with city recreational departments, youth centers and other nonprofit organizations to deliver enrichment programs that empower students to pursue academic goals and have a positive outlook on their futures. GEMS is excited to now also offer our programs directly with local schools and districts. For more information on our current programs, click here.

Of course, the village is much larger than just the GEMS Inc. organization. We strive to collaborate within our network of community service providers and organizations that share our agenda for benefiting the community.

If you share our vision and would like to volunteer or collaborate, please feel free to contact us.
